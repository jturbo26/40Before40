Day A · Push + Legs · ~20 min
Legs
Goblet Squat Wide Stance PR: 60lb
2 sets × 10 reps · 50–60 lbs · toes out, sink deep
Hold dumbbell vertically at chest. Feet wider than shoulder width, toes pointed out 45°. Sit straight down as deep as you can — knees track over toes. Your legs are your strongest muscle group right now. Own it.
Push
Incline DB Bench Press PR: 45lb
2 sets × 8–10 reps · 35–40 lbs · neutral grip, watch shoulder
Bench at 30–45°, palms facing each other (neutral grip). Elbows at 45° to your body — not flared out. Stop if you feel shoulder impingement. Drop weight before dropping reps.
Seated Overhead Press PR: 30lb
2 sets × 10 reps · 25–30 lbs
Sit upright, core tight. Press straight up, don't let the weights drift forward. Lower controlled — don't drop them. Shoulder friendly as long as you don't arch your back excessively.
Shoulders + Core
Lateral Raise PR: 15lb
2 sets × 10–12 reps · 10–15 lbs
Slight bend in elbows, raise arms out to the side to shoulder height. Lead with your elbows, not your hands. Slow and controlled on the way down — that's where the work happens.
Hollow Hold PR: 39 sec
2 sets · 35–40 sec
Lie on your back, lower back pressed flat into the floor — that's the key. Arms extended overhead, legs straight and raised. If your back lifts off, bend your knees. You hit 39 seconds in peak week. Hold that.
Day A Notes
Shoulder: Drop weight before dropping reps if it flares up.
Goblet squat: Stack a dumbbell on 50 to hit 55–60 if needed.
Goal: Maintain intensity, not volume. 2 heavy sets is enough.
Day B · Pull + Hinge · ~20 min
Hinge
Dumbbell Deadlift PR: 60lb
2 sets × 10 reps · 50–60 lbs · hinge at hips
Hinge at the hips, not the knees. Push your butt back, keep your back flat, feel the stretch in your hamstrings. Drive through your heels to stand. This is where your form clicked in week 3 — keep that feeling.
Dumbbell Glute Bridge PR: 40lb
2 sets × 15 reps · 35–40 lbs
Lie on your back, feet flat on floor hip-width apart. Dumbbell on your hips. Drive hips straight up, squeeze your glutes hard at the top. Lower controlled. Higher reps here — feel the burn.
Pull
Chest Supported DB Row PR: 50lb
2 sets × 10 reps · 40–50 lbs
Bench at 45°, chest against the pad. Arms hang straight down. Row both dumbbells up toward your hips, squeeze shoulder blades together at the top. Chest stays on the pad — no cheating with momentum.
Standing Dumbbell Curl PR: 25lb
2 sets × 12 reps · 20–25 lbs
Stand tall, elbows pinned at your sides — they don't move. Curl up, squeeze at the top, lower slow. Alternate arms or do both together. Don't swing your body to cheat the weight up.
Triceps + Core
Tricep Extension PR: 35lb
2 sets × 12 reps · 25–30 lbs
Seated, hold one dumbbell with both hands overhead. Lower it behind your head, elbows pointing forward and staying still. Press back up to full extension. The only thing moving is your forearms.
Lying Leg Raise PR: 15 reps
2 sets × 12–15 reps · bodyweight
Flat on your back, hands under your lower back for support. Legs straight, raise them to 90° then lower slowly — don't let them touch the floor. The slower the negative, the harder it works.
Day B Notes
Deadlift: Hinge, don't squat. Feel it in hamstrings.
Row: Chest supported — no cheating. Control the negative.
Goal: Don't let the row drop below 40 lbs.
